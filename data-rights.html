Permissions We Request & Why

Our app requests certain permissions to function properly. Here's exactly why we need each one:

READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
To access your photo gallery so you can select images to edit. We only read images you explicitly choose.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
To save edited images back to your device's gallery so you can keep your creations. This is only used for saving your final edited photos.
INTERNET & NETWORK_STATE
To display advertisements (if enabled) and check connectivity. No personal data or images are transmitted for ads.
CAMERA (Optional)
Only if you choose to take new photos directly within the app instead of selecting from gallery.
