IMPORTANT PRIVACY NOTICE

AI Photo Enhancer processes all images 100% locally on your device. Your photos never leave your phone, tablet, or computer. We do not upload, store, or access your photos on any servers. The only internet connection used by our app is for displaying advertisements through approved ad networks.

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Photo Enhancer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "Application").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Application.

1.1 Local Processing Commitment

We want to emphasize our core privacy principle: All photo processing occurs locally on your device. The artificial intelligence models that enhance your photos are downloaded to your device and run entirely on your device's processor. No image data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers during the enhancement process.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect minimal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Photos you select for enhancement: These photos are processed exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • App preferences and settings: Settings you choose within the Application, such as default enhancement options.
  • Optional feedback: If you choose to provide feedback or contact support, we may collect your email address and the content of your message.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Application, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about the mobile device you use to access our Application, including the device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with our Application, such as which features you use most frequently, session duration, and crash reports to improve app stability.
  • Advertisement Information: To display relevant ads, our advertising partners may collect information about your device and usage patterns. This is governed by their respective privacy policies.

2.3 What We DO NOT Collect

To provide complete transparency, we explicitly state what we do NOT collect:

  • We do NOT collect or access your photos. All photos remain on your device.
  • We do NOT upload your photos to any server.
  • We do NOT use your photos for training our AI models.
  • We do NOT share your photos with third parties.
  • We do NOT store your photos in the cloud.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To Provide the Service: To enable the photo enhancement features that run locally on your device.
  • To Improve Our Application: To understand how users interact with our Application and to improve its performance, features, and user experience.
  • To Display Advertisements: To support our free version of the Application through advertisements. Internet connection is required only for this purpose.
  • To Communicate with You: To respond to your inquiries, provide support, and send you important notices regarding the Application.
  • For Security Purposes: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or potentially prohibited or illegal activities.

4. Local Processing Architecture

Our Application is designed with privacy-first architecture:

4.1 On-Device AI Processing

The core functionality of AI Photo Enhancer relies on neural networks that run entirely on your device. When you enhance a photo:

  1. The photo is loaded into your device's memory
  2. Our AI models (already downloaded to your device) process the image
  3. The enhanced result is saved to your device's storage
  4. All intermediate data is cleared from memory
  5. No data is transmitted over the internet during this process

4.2 Offline Functionality

The Application's photo enhancement features work completely offline. You do not need an internet connection to enhance your photos. Internet connection is only required for:

  • Downloading the Application and updates
  • Displaying advertisements (in the free version)
  • Optional feedback submission

4.3 Data Storage

All photos, both original and enhanced, are stored only on your device in locations you designate (typically your device's gallery or photos app). We do not have access to these storage locations.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the minimal information we collect. However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6.1 Your Device's Security

Since all photo processing occurs on your device, the security of your photos depends largely on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Keeping your operating system updated
  • Only installing apps from trusted sources
